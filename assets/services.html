Our Services

Our mission is to become the leading digital dental lab in Sweden and put us on the front line of innovation. We work with the latest CAD software and machinery. Not only that, but, we use only ISO-certified materials to provide high-quality smile makeovers.

The process goes in a few stages. Below you can see the timeline of events that take place.

Consultation. Takes place in the lab or online via Zoom. We discuss possibilities that are individual to the patient.
Picture Taking High-quality pictures are necessary for the most realistic outcome.
2D Smile Design Based on patients' desires and expectations, we create a preview of a smile-makeover on a 2D picture. We like to call it "The Ideal Smile".
3D Mock-Up Design Our technicians create a 3D-printed model of the "Ideal Smile".
Color sampling We take the color of teeth in the lab to ensure matching with patients' teeth. At the patient's request, we can change it to any other desired color.
3D Smile Design Aesthetic design performed in CAD Software.
Production With the newest machinery, such as 3D printers, CNC-carving machines, and high-quality aesthetic materials that comply with ISO standards. We turn the 3D design into physical form.
Finalization We work with an esthetician who adds the final touch to the construction.

For any additional questions, feel free to reach out, we will advise you the best as we can.
