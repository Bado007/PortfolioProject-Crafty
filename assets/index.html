Welcome to Crafty Lab

Crafty Lab, opened its doors in 2018. We are a full-service dental laboratory with a strong commitment to keeping up with the latest technology in dentistry. We are a family-owned business that prides itself on exemplary quality and excellent customer service. Furthermore, we have CAD/CAM capabilities on-site and can design crowns, bridges, abutments, diagnostic wax-ups and more for your practice specialized in aesthetics. We accept intramural scans from all major digital impression systems.
